Data Collection

We are committed to minimising data collection. This website:

  • Does not use cookies – no tracking cookies, no session cookies, no cookie banners needed.
  • Does not use analytics – no Google Analytics, no Matomo, no third-party tracking of any kind.
  • Does not load external fonts – all fonts are hosted locally on our server. No requests are sent to Google Fonts or other external font services.
  • Does not embed external resources – no third-party scripts, pixels, or widgets are loaded.

Your language preference (EN/DE) is stored in your browser’s localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Contact

When you contact us by email, the data you provide (your email address, name, and message content) will be stored and processed for the purpose of handling your enquiry. We will not share this data with third parties without your consent.

  • Right to rectification – You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
