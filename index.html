don't reach Europe.

Held at the border Machines detained at EU customs because CE documentation doesn't match the new Machinery Regulation.
Documentation gaps Risk assessments, technical files and declarations that don't meet EU requirements.

Our Services

From assessment to CE mark.

We combine engineering expertise with deep knowledge of EU regulation — so your documentation meets every requirement.

01 — CE Gap Analysis

We review your existing documentation against EU requirements and show exactly what's missing. Your low-risk first step.

02 — Risk Assessment

Full hazard analysis to ISO 12100 (and ISO 10218 / ISO/TS 15066 for collaborative robots) — the legal core of CE.

03 — Technical Dossier

We compile and write the complete technical file, so you can issue the Declaration of Conformity and affix CE.

04 — Ongoing Compliance

Your dedicated EU-compliance partner for a whole product range, on a fixed monthly basis.

05 — Training & Workshops

We train your engineering and export team on EU requirements — on-site or online.
