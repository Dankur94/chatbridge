Company

What we do

BitsBridge is a systems engineering company based in Hong Kong. We build custom technical solutions — software, hardware, and the integrations between both. With direct access to the Greater Bay Area's electronics ecosystem, we source components, build prototypes, and deliver faster than traditional agencies.

Our team operates across two locations: development and hardware sourcing on the ground in Hong Kong and the Greater Bay Area — Asia's super-connector and the gateway for Western businesses into mainland China, offering preferential market access under "One Country, Two Systems" and the world's densest electronics manufacturing ecosystem at our doorstep. Client consulting and technical analysis from Aachen, Germany — at the Dreiländereck where Germany, the Netherlands, and Belgium meet, home to RWTH Aachen, and surrounded by R&D centers of Ford, Siemens, and Covestro — one of Europe's most concentrated clusters of engineering science and industrial innovation.
