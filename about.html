What we do

BitsBridge is a systems engineering company based in Hong Kong. We build custom technical solutions — software, hardware, and the integrations between both. With direct access to Shenzhen's electronics ecosystem, we source components, build prototypes, and deliver faster than traditional agencies.

Our team operates across two locations: development and hardware sourcing on the ground in Hong Kong and Shenzhen, client consulting and technical analysis in Germany. AI tools are our force multiplier — we deliver in days what agencies take weeks for.
