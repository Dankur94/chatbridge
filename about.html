Our Story

Our Journey

It started in 2017.

Two students at RWTH Aachen — one engineer, one physicist — navigating the same hurdles.

We became friends. Flatmates. Each other's mentors. We shared everything — lecture notes, late nights, and eventually, a vision.

What we had in common: an obsession with getting to the root of things. Never the surface. Always the why.

Ananya found it in physics. Complex systems, broken down to their deepest layer, then explained so anyone could understand. The joy of making the invisible visible.

Daniel found it in building. Hands on hardware. Turning chaos into structure. Giving order to data. Automating what shouldn't be done by hand.

Two different paths. One shared instinct: understand first, then solve.

That's why we started BitsBridge.
