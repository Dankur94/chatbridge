What we do

BitsBridge builds custom technical solutions for companies with China business — software, hardware, and the bridge between both. We combine engineering expertise from RWTH Aachen with direct access to Shenzhen's manufacturing ecosystem.

Our team covers the full chain: requirements analysis and client consulting in Germany, development and hardware sourcing in Hong Kong and Shenzhen. AI tools are our force multiplier — we deliver in days what agencies take weeks for.
